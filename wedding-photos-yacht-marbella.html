Wedding photos on a boat in Marbella

Booking a session for wedding photos boat Marbella couples remember for a lifetime means trading the crowded promenade for a private deck, a glassy sea and the Costa del Sol skyline behind you. Aboard our De Antonio D50 you and your photographer have the whole yacht to yourselves, sailing out of Puerto Banús at the exact hour the light turns gold. No queues, no strangers in frame, no compromise on the backdrop.

Whether it is your wedding day, a pre-wedding story or a romantic anniversary, we handle the logistics so you can focus on each other and the camera.

The perfect shoot onboard

Timing is everything. We plan your departure around golden hour, the soft, warm window before sunset when the light flatters every skin tone and turns the water into liquid amber. It is the same magic that makes our sunset tour off Marbella so popular, only here it is dedicated entirely to your images.

The bay offers three distinct backdrops in a single outing. Start against the glamorous marina of Puerto Banús, with its superyachts and palm-lined quay. Drift along the Golden Mile, where the coastline and mountains frame the couple beautifully. Then head to open sea, where nothing but horizon surrounds you for those clean, cinematic wide shots. Your photographer can work bow, stern and sundeck as the yacht turns to chase the best angle of the sun.

What's included

Every session comes with a professional skipper at the helm, all fuel and dedicated on-water time for the shoot, so nothing eats into your golden hour. The skipper knows where to position the boat for even light and calm water, and will hold or reposition whenever your photographer needs a particular frame.

You are free to bring your own photographer and videographer aboard, or we can recommend a trusted local professional used to shooting on a moving deck. Add floral styling or a bottle of champagne for a few celebratory frames.

For newlyweds & post-wedding

The deck is a wonderful place to wear the dress a second time. A trash-the-dress shoot lets you swap the formality of the ceremony for salt air, bare feet and unguarded laughter, and the results are always more relaxed and playful than the wedding-day set. Couples celebrating an anniversary come back to mark the occasion in the same light, and many book a pre-wedding session weeks before the big day for save-the-dates and invitations.

If you are still dreaming up the moment itself, the yacht also makes an unforgettable stage for a marriage proposal off Marbella, with a discreet photographer ready to capture the yes.

Frequently asked questions

How long does a wedding photo shoot on the boat take?

A two-hour private charter is usually enough for a complete wedding photos boat Marbella session, covering Puerto Banús, the Golden Mile and open water. If you want a longer edit or want to combine the shoot with a relaxed cruise, we can extend the time to suit.

Do we bring our own photographer or do you provide one?

Both work. You are welcome to bring your own photographer and videographer aboard, or we can recommend a trusted local professional who knows how to shoot in strong Mediterranean light and on a moving boat.

When is the best time of day for the photos?

Golden hour, the last hour or two before sunset, gives the warmest and most flattering light. We plan the departure around it so your images are bathed in soft golden tones rather than harsh midday glare.

Can we do a trash-the-dress or anniversary session?

Yes. The De Antonio D50 is ideal for post-wedding trash-the-dress shoots, pre-wedding sessions and anniversary photos, so you can wear the dress again in a completely different setting on the water.
