Book a hen party boat in Marbella the easy way

Planning a hen party boat Marbella weekend and want the bride to remember it for all the right reasons? A private day on our De Antonio D50 gives your group the Mediterranean to itself: no shared decks, no strangers, no fixed timetable. You board in the heart of Puerto Banús, choose the coves you fancy, and your skipper handles the navigation while you focus on the celebration. It slots neatly around lunch, spa bookings or a night out in Banús. For timings, extras and packing tips, our full hen party boat Marbella guide walks you through every detail before you pick a date.

The perfect hen party plan on the water

Most groups build the day around three moments: a scenic cruise, a swim, and a toast. We usually suggest a late-afternoon departure so you glide out along the Golden Mile while the light softens, then drop anchor in a sheltered bay for swimming, paddle boarding and photos. As the sun dips, the deck becomes the ideal spot for a chilled glass of cava and a toast to the bride. If a sunset cruise in Marbella is the mood you are after, we time the itinerary so you are anchored at exactly the right hour. Prefer a livelier daytime party? A midday slot leaves plenty of time for longer swim stops, music and lunch on board.

Music, drinks & catering onboard

The boat comes with a Bluetooth sound system, so your playlist runs the show from the moment you cast off. Bring your own cava, soft drinks and nibbles, or let us arrange catering and iced drinks so everything is cold when you arrive. Decoration is welcome too, whether that means balloons, sashes or a personalised banner for the bride, and we set it up before boarding. Want to go all out? We can add a jet ski, a professional photographer or a floating lounger. There is a fridge on board and shaded seating so the party keeps its energy from the first swim to the last song.

Group size & price

The De Antonio D50 carries up to 11 guests, so a typical hen group travels together on one private boat. Pricing is per boat, not per person, which makes it easy to share the cost. A two-hour cruise is €1,200, four hours is €1,800, and a full day is €3,000. Every option includes your skipper and fuel, and we recommend four hours as the sweet spot: enough time to cruise, swim, take photos and toast without feeling rushed. Extras are added on request when you confirm your date.

Frequently asked questions

How many people can join a hen party boat in Marbella?

Up to 11 guests can come aboard the De Antonio D50, so the bride and her friends travel together on one private boat rather than being split across several vessels.

What is included in the hen party price?

Every charter includes your private skipper, fuel, the onboard sound system, a paddle board and snorkelling gear. You only add the extras you want, such as catering, decoration or a jet ski.

Can we bring our own drinks and decorations?

Yes. You are welcome to bring your own cava, soft drinks and hen party decorations, or ask us to arrange catering and styling before you arrive so the boat is ready when you board.

When is the best time for a hen party cruise?

Late afternoon into the golden hour is the most popular slot, combining warm swim stops with a sunset toast, while morning departures are calmer and cooler for groups who prefer a relaxed start.
