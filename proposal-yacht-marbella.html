Why choose a marriage proposal boat in Marbella

Few settings rival the Mediterranean at dusk for popping the question, and a marriage proposal boat Marbella charter gives you the open sea, the Puerto Banús skyline and a golden horizon entirely to yourselves. Aboard our private De Antonio D50 there are no crowded restaurants and no prying eyes — just the two of you, a discreet skipper and a moment arranged down to the smallest detail. If you would like to picture how the afternoon unfolds, our guide to a marriage proposal at sea walks you through every step.

How we arrange the proposal

Everything is planned in secret over WhatsApp, so your partner suspects nothing. We dress the deck with romantic decoration and fresh flowers, chill a bottle of champagne and cue the music you choose for the big moment. Our skipper knows exactly when to ease off the throttle, step back and give you privacy, then times the whole thing for the sunset, when the light over Marbella is at its most flattering. You simply tell us the words you want to say; we take care of the rest.

Romantic extras

Want to remember every second? An onboard photographer can stay hidden until the "yes", then capture the celebration as you cruise back to port — the same team behind our wedding photo sessions. We can also rig a personalised "Will you marry me?" banner, add a tailored catering platter with cava and canapés, or arrange live acoustic music. Tell us your ideas and we will price them into your quote.

Who and when

Most couples choose an intimate charter for two, but the D50 comfortably seats a small group if you would like family or close friends waiting to surprise the newlyweds-to-be. Proposals work beautifully all year round on the Costa del Sol, though late spring through early autumn brings the warmest evenings. Whatever the season, we always aim for the hour before sunset — the most magical window of the day.

Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend at least a week so we can source flowers and confirm your preferred date, but we will always try to accommodate last-minute requests.

Will my partner know beforehand?

No. All planning happens directly with you, and the skipper is briefed to keep the surprise until your chosen moment.

What happens if the weather turns?

If conditions are unsafe we will reschedule at no extra cost or agree a full refund — your safety and the moment come first.

Can we stay out to celebrate afterwards?

Absolutely. Your two hours are yours to enjoy, so raise a glass and cruise the coast once the ring is on.
