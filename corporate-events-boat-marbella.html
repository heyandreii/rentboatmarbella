Corporate events boat Marbella: business, above the waterline

When you organise a corporate events boat Marbella outing aboard our private De Antonio D50, you swap the meeting room for the open Mediterranean off Puerto Banús. There are no neighbouring tables, no interruptions and no fixed schedule but yours. A single company or team has the whole yacht, the skipper and the deck to itself, which makes it easy to talk business, reward a result or simply give colleagues and clients a few hours they will actually remember.

The format is flexible: a focused two-hour session or a full day along the Costa del Sol, with catering, timing and route shaped around your agenda.

Ideal for incentives & team building

A yacht is one of the few settings that works equally well for an informal meeting and a proper celebration. Use it as an incentive: a reward for the team that hit its targets, where the sea and a glass of cava do more for morale than any bonus slide. Use it for team building, where a smaller group relaxes, talks openly and comes back closer than they left. Or use it to close the quarter, marking a launch, a signed contract or a milestone worth acknowledging out loud.

It also quietly impresses clients. Hosting a prospect or a long-standing partner on a private deck off Marbella says more about how you value the relationship than a restaurant booking ever could, and the relaxed setting tends to move conversations forward.

What's included & options

Every charter includes a professional skipper, fuel, insurance and full on-board coordination, so the day runs without you managing the logistics. From there you build the experience: extended catering and premium drinks, a photographer, branded décor for a product moment, and private transfers to and from the marina. Discretion is part of the service: sensitive conversations stay firmly on board.

Because these are genuine business outings, we issue a proper VAT invoice for your accounts. Tell us the guest count and the date and we will prepare a bespoke proposal. You can compare specifications across the fleet, and if the occasion is more of a private party, our birthday boat charter covers the celebration side.

What kind of event fits best

The D50 carries up to ten guests, so it suits small teams and intimate hospitality rather than large conferences. That cap keeps the day personal: think a leadership offsite, an incentive for a top-performing unit, a private setting to host two or three VIP clients, or a relaxed networking session where the right people can actually hear one another. If your headcount is higher, ask us and we will advise.

Frequently asked questions

Do you provide a VAT invoice for the company?

Yes. Every corporate charter is invoiced with VAT and your company details, so the outing can be booked and expensed through your accounts like any other business service.

How many people can join a corporate event on board?

The De Antonio D50 takes up to ten guests, which makes it ideal for small teams, leadership offsites and hosting VIP clients rather than large conferences.

Can you arrange catering and drinks?

Certainly. We offer extended catering, premium drinks and bespoke menus, plus extras such as a photographer, branded décor and private transfers, all tailored to your agenda.

How far in advance should we book?

As early as you can during the summer season, when dates fill quickly. Share your preferred date and guest count and we will confirm availability and a bespoke proposal, usually within two hours.
