The best birthday boat trip Marbella has to offer

A birthday boat trip Marbella style means swapping the crowded restaurant for the open sea, with the skyline of Puerto Banús behind you and the whole yacht reserved for your group alone. Aboard our De Antonio D50 you set the mood: your own playlist through the sound system, a chilled bottle to toast the moment, and warm, glassy water waiting the second the anchor drops. It is a celebration that feels effortless, private and genuinely unforgettable.

From the first minute to the last, the day is built around the birthday guest. There is no fixed script, no shared deck and no rush — just a beautiful boat, a friendly skipper and the freedom to celebrate exactly how you like along the Costa del Sol.

The perfect birthday plan

We cast off from Puerto Banús and cruise the bay while the music plays and the first drinks are poured. When we reach a sheltered cove, the skipper drops anchor so everyone can dive into crystal-clear water, float on the paddle board or snorkel over the rocks. It is the heart of the day: laughter, splashing and a sunbathing platform all to yourselves.

Back on board, we bring out the cake and raise a glass for the birthday toast — candles, singing and photos with the sea as your backdrop. As the afternoon softens, we time the return so you can watch the golden sunset over the water before gliding back into port. Swim, music, cake and sunset: four simple moments that turn an ordinary birthday into a story people retell for years.

What's included & the extras

Every private charter comes with a professional skipper, all fuel for your route, and the water toys already on board — paddle board, snorkelling gear and a swim platform. Light refreshments are included too, so you can step aboard and simply begin celebrating without a single thing to organise.

Want to make it extra special? We arrange birthday decoration and balloons, a cake ready with candles, and full catering from tapas to champagne. For a hit of adrenaline you can add a jet ski to the day. Tell us the vibe you are after and we will have it all prepared before you arrive, whether it is a laid-back afternoon or a lively group party on the water.

A birthday for all ages

The beauty of a private yacht is that it fits any birthday. Children's parties come alive when a pod of dolphins appears alongside the hull, and the shallow swim stops and paddle board keep younger guests entertained for hours. The skipper sets a gentle pace and keeps a close eye on everyone in the water.

For adults, it is the ultimate milestone celebration — a 30th, 40th or 50th spent sailing with the people who matter most. We are also happy to help with surprise birthdays: secret decoration, a hidden cake and a perfectly timed toast, all coordinated quietly with you in advance so the big reveal lands beautifully.

Frequently asked questions

How many people can join a birthday boat trip in Marbella?

The De Antonio D50 carries up to 10 guests on a fully private charter, which makes it ideal for an intimate group of friends or the whole family celebrating together.

Can we bring a cake and decorations on board?

Yes. You are welcome to bring your own cake, and we can also arrange a cake, balloons and decoration for you as an extra so everything is set up before you step aboard.

Is a birthday boat trip suitable for children?

Absolutely. Children love spotting dolphins, swimming from the platform and using the paddle board. The skipper adjusts the pace and swim stops to suit younger guests.

How far in advance should we book a birthday charter?

We recommend booking one to two weeks ahead in summer to secure your preferred date and time, though we often accommodate last-minute requests. We confirm every booking in under two hours.
