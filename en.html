Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to rent a boat in Marbella?

Our private departures from Puerto Banús are €1,200 (2h), €1,800 (4h) and €3,000 (8h), with skipper, fuel and equipment included. No surprises.

Do I need a boat licence?

No. Every trip includes a licensed skipper; you just enjoy the day.

What's included in the price?

Skipper, fuel, insurance, paddle board, snorkel and light catering on board.

What happens if the weather is bad?

If the skipper judges the sea unsafe, we cancel and refund 100%. You can also change the date free of charge up to 24h ahead.
