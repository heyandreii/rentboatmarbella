Why book direct

Private boat hire vs rental platforms in Marbella

Updated July 2026 · 5 min read

Booking private boat hire in Marbella directly with the people who operate the boat is not the same as going through a large nautical marketplace. When you book direct you speak to the team who will be on board, shape the route around your day and pay exactly what you see, with no layers of middlemen. In this honest comparison we set out the real differences — without running anyone down — so you can decide with full information which option suits your day at sea.

100% private, never shared

The fundamental difference is who steps aboard with you. On a direct private charter, the boat is yours alone: you, your family or your friends, and nobody else. You choose the music, the swim stops and the pace of the day.

On many platforms, the option that looks cheapest is often a shared trip: you climb aboard with strangers, on a fixed schedule and a set route you cannot change. It is a perfectly valid formula for anyone after a quick outing, but it completely changes the experience if you are celebrating something or want privacy. With us you sail aboard the De Antonio D50 exclusively, for groups of up to 11 people.

Multilingual professional skipper included

When you book direct you know in advance that a professional skipper is on board and which language they will look after you in. Ours speaks English, Spanish, French and Russian, knows every cove between Puerto Banús and Cabopino, and knows where the water is at its most turquoise depending on the day's wind.

On a generic marketplace it is not always clear whether the price includes a skipper, or whether the boat comes bareboat and you have to helm it yourself with your own licence. That detail dramatically changes who can enjoy the day, and how. With a local skipper you also pick up recommendations that appear on no listing: the best moment for a sunset tour, where the dolphins tend to appear, or which beach club to book on the way back.

All-inclusive, transparent pricing

When you book direct, the price you see is the final price: skipper, fuel for the usual route and VAT included, with no platform commissions or service charges appearing at the last step of checkout. These are our rates for the whole boat, not per person:

Full refund if the sea makes sailing unsafe

The weather rules at sea, which is why we offer a clear guarantee: if conditions do not allow us to sail safely, we reschedule your trip or give you a full refund. Because you deal with us directly, we make that decision together, with no intermediary in between.

With a booking through a third party, by contrast, the weather cancellation policy depends on the marketplace and does not always match the boat operator's own terms, which can complicate arranging a refund. Booking direct removes that uncertainty.

Direct contact and flexibility

Booking direct means having a real person to talk to. You message us on WhatsApp, we answer questions on the spot, adjust the departure time if your group is running late, and adapt the plan if one day you fancy quiet coves and another something livelier.

That closeness shows in the details too: we suggest the best coves to drop anchor, coordinate catering or decorations for a celebration, and tell you what to expect depending on the time of year. It is the kind of support a standard platform form rarely provides. When you are ready, booking your private charter takes a couple of minutes.

Frequently asked questions

Is a private boat the same as booking on a platform?

No. On a 100% private charter the boat is yours alone, with a flexible route and timing. On many marketplaces the cheapest option is a shared trip with strangers and a fixed schedule.

A multilingual professional skipper, fuel for the usual route and VAT, with no platform fees. From €1,200 for 2 hours, €1,800 for a half day (4h) and €3,000 for a full day.

What happens if the sea makes sailing unsafe?

If conditions do not allow us to sail safely, we reschedule your trip or give you a full refund. Booking direct means you are not tied to an intermediary's refund policy.
